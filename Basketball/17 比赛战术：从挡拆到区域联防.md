# 17 比赛战术：从挡拆到区域联防

## Opening Scene

> "Warriors run a **split action** off the **horns set**. Curry comes off a **double screen**, **flares to the wing**, catches and... **pump fakes**, **drives baseline**, **kicks out** to Klay in the corner. **Splash!** That's **motion offense** at its finest."

战术是篮球的灵魂。从最基础的 pick-and-roll，到现代的 motion offense、5-out 阵型，再到 zone defense 的各种变体——这一篇把战术术语全部拆解。

## 战术体系 Tactical System

```
进攻战术 (Offensive Schemes)
    ↓
防守战术 (Defensive Schemes)
    ↓
战术执行 (Execution)
    ↓
临场调整 (Adjustments)
```

> "They **switch everything**—no **mismatches** to exploit."
> 他们全部换防——没有错位可利用。

> "Big man in **drop coverage**, guard **goes over** the screen."
> 大个沉退，后卫从掩护上绕过。

**挡拆防守对比**：
- **Switch** = 互换防守对象（避免错位防守）
- **Hedge/Show** = 大个短暂阻拦持球人后回防自己人
- **Drop** = 大个直接退到篮下，让后卫单防持球人
- **Trap/Blitz** = 双人包夹持球人
- **Ice** = 把持球人逼向边线（边线挡拆专用）

## Listen & Learn

一段完整战术解说：

> "Down by 2, **15 seconds** left, **out of timeout**. Coach **draws up a play**: **horns set**, Curry at the top. **Stagger screen** from Draymond and Klay, Curry **comes off**, **catches at the wing**. Defender **goes under**, Curry **pulls up from 30 feet**... **Splash!** Three-pointer to win the game! That's the **bread and butter**, that's **late-game execution** at its finest."

拆解：
- **out of timeout** = 暂停回来
- **draws up a play** = 画战术
- **horns set** = 牛角站位
- **stagger screen** = 错位双掩护
- **comes off** = 借掩护出来
- **goes under** = 从掩护下方绕
- **pulls up** = 急停跳投
- **bread and butter** = 看家本领
- **late-game execution** = 末段执行

翻译：落后2分，剩15秒，暂停回来。教练画出战术：牛角站位，库里在弧顶。追梦和克莱错位双掩护，库里借掩护出来，侧翼接球。防守人从掩护下方绕，库里30尺急停跳投……进！绝杀三分！这就是看家本领，这就是顶级末段执行。
