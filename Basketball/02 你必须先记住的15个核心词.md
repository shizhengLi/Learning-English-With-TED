# 02 你必须先记住的15个核心词

## Opening Scene

> "Curry brings the ball up court, passes to Green at the top of the key. Green drives, dishes to Thompson on the wing — he shoots the three... BANG!"

如果你只听懂了 Curry、Green、Thompson 三个人名，别急——这一篇帮你把最高频的15个词搞定，下次再听这段，每个动作都能跟上。

## Deep Dive：这些词为什么重要？

### 1. 技术统计全靠它们

打开任何一个 box score（技术统计表），你会看到这些缩写：

| 缩写 | 全称           | 对应上面第几个词 |
| ---- | -------------- | ---------------- |
| PTS  | Points (score) | #5               |
| AST  | Assists        | #6               |
| REB  | Rebounds       | #4               |
| STL  | Steals         | #7               |
| BLK  | Blocks         | #8               |
| TO   | Turnovers      | #9               |
| FT   | Free Throws    | #11              |

**一条实用规律**：NBA 统计缩写几乎都是单词的前三个字母。知道全称就能猜出缩写。

### 2. shoot / pass / dribble 是三大基础动作

英文解说用这三个词衍生出几十个搭配：

- shoot → shooter, shot, shooting guard, pull-up shot, catch-and-shoot
- pass → passer, passing lane, no-look pass, bounce pass, dish
- dribble → dribbler, crossover dribble, between-the-legs dribble

你记住了根词，后面碰到搭配就不怕。

### 3. "dish" 是 "pass" 的口语替身

解说员嫌 pass 太普通，经常换成 dish：

> "He **dishes** it to the corner — three-pointer, good!"

类似的口语替换还有：
- score → bucket, put it in
- dunk → slam, throw it down, jam
- block → swat, reject

这就是你以后会在第30篇"解说金句"里集中学到的内容，现在先知道有这回事。

## Listen & Learn

回到开头那段解说，逐词拆解：

> "Curry **brings the ball up court**（运球过半场）, **passes** to Green at the top of the key（传球给罚球弧顶的Green）. Green **drives**（突破）, **dishes** to Thompson on the wing（分球给侧翼的Thompson）— he **shoots** the three（投三分）... BANG!"

现在是不是清楚多了？

再来一段：

> "James gets the **steal**, pushes it in transition, rises up and throws down a monster **dunk**! And one — he's fouled!"

翻译：James 完成抢断，推快攻，起跳扣篮！而且加罚——他被犯规了！

这里的 **and one** 是篮球专属说法：投篮命中的同时被犯规，加罚一球。记住它，出现频率极高。

## Practice 小练习

**Ⅰ. 英译中（用上面15个词翻译）**
1. He scored 30 points with 8 assists.
2. The ref called a foul, so he went to the free throw line.
3. She grabbed 15 rebounds in the first three quarters.

**Ⅱ. 中译英**
1. 他运球过了半场，投了一记三分。
2. 第四节还剩2分钟，教练叫了暂停。
3. 加时赛他扣篮绝杀。

**Ⅲ. 连线：把口语说法和标准说法配对**

| 口语   | 标准  |
| ------ | ----- |
| dish   | block |
| bucket | pass  |
| swat   | score |

---

**参考答案**

- Ⅰ.
  1. 他拿了30分8助攻。
  2. 裁判吹了犯规，所以他走上罚球线。
  3. 前三节她抢下了15个篮板。

- Ⅱ.
  1. He dribbled across half court and shot a three.
  2. With 2 minutes left in the fourth quarter, the coach called a timeout.
  3. He dunked the game-winner in overtime.

- Ⅲ. dish = pass / bucket = score / swat = block
