# 18 教练与训练：从战术板到训练场

## Opening Scene

> "He's the **head coach**, but it takes a **whole staff**: **assistant coaches**, **player development coaches**, **video coordinators**, **strength and conditioning coaches**. Behind every great team is a great **coaching staff**, **drawing up plays**, **breaking down film**, **running drills**, building **culture**."

教练是球队的大脑。从主帅到助教，从战术板到训练场，从录像分析到球员培养——这一篇带你深入 NBA 教练组的运作。

## 教练体系 Coaching System

```
教练组架构 (Coaching Staff)
    ↓
战术准备 (Tactical Preparation)
    ↓
训练管理 (Training Management)
    ↓
比赛执教 (Game Coaching)
```

## Listen & Learn

一段完整教练场景：

> "It's **2 AM**, and Coach is in the **film room**, **breaking down film** of tomorrow's opponent. **Scouting report** on his desk. He's looking for **tendencies**, **mismatches**, **plays to exploit**. At **9 AM**, he runs **morning shootaround**—**walkthrough** of the **game plan**. **Pregame**, he gives a **fiery speech**, **rallies the troops**. During the game, he **draws up plays out of timeouts**, **tinkers with the rotation**, and in the fourth quarter, he **shortens the bench**, **rides the hot hand**, and **calls the right play** for the **game winner**. That's **NBA coaching**."

拆解：
- **breaking down film** = 拆解录像
- **tendencies** = 习惯
- **mismatches** = 错位
- **walkthrough** = 走战术
- **rallies the troops** = 鼓舞士气
- **draws up plays out of timeouts** = 暂停画战术
- **tinkers with the rotation** = 调整轮换
- **shortens the bench** = 缩短轮换
- **rides the hot hand** = 用手热球员

翻译：凌晨2点，教练在录像室拆解明天对手录像。球探报告摆桌上。他在找习惯、错位、可利用的战术。早9点，他主持早投——走一遍比赛计划。赛前，他发表激昂讲话，鼓舞士气。比赛中，他暂停时画战术，调整轮换，第四节缩短轮换，用手热球员，关键时刻安排正确战术拿下绝杀。这就是NBA执教。
